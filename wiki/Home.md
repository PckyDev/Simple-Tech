# Simple Tech Wiki

Welcome to the Simple Tech wiki.

This wiki documents the full Simple Tech ecosystem as it exists on 2026-03-07.

## What is Simple Tech?

Simple Tech is a modular Skript-based automation and progression system for vanilla Minecraft servers. It adds:

- Vanilla-style custom items
- Machine processing chains
- Heat, fuel, and pseudo-power systems
- Logistics and virtual buffers
- Advanced machine progression
- Ownership, access, and admin tooling
- Balance, recovery, and migration helpers

It is designed to feel like lightweight industrial gameplay without requiring a resource pack or custom blocks.
