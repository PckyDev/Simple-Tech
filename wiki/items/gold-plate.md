- **Tier:** Primitive Tech
- **Base item:** Light Weighted Pressure Plate

## How to Obtain
### Anvil Processing
- `Hammer + Gold Ingot` → `Gold Plate`

### Press Processing
- `Gold Ingot` in front of a powered [Press](../machines/press.md) → `2x Gold Plate`
