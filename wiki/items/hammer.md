# Hammer

## Summary
- **Tier:** Primitive Tech
- **Base item:** Mace
- **Purpose:** Manual processing tool for early progression, with optional mining upgrades through custom enchantments

## Crafting Recipe
```text
[Iron Ingot] [Iron Ingot] [Iron Ingot]
[Iron Ingot] [Stick]      [Iron Ingot]
[Empty]      [Stick]      [Empty]
```

## Uses
- In an anvil with raw ore to make crushed ore.
- In an anvil with an ingot to make a plate.
- Not consumed during processing.
- Cannot be used as a weapon.
- Can be enchanted with the custom `Excavation` enchantment.

## Processing Recipes
- `Hammer + Raw Iron` → `2x Crushed Iron`
- `Hammer + Raw Gold` → `2x Crushed Gold`
- `Hammer + Raw Copper` → `2x Crushed Copper`
- `Hammer + Iron Ingot` → `Iron Plate`
- `Hammer + Gold Ingot` → `Gold Plate`
- `Hammer + Copper Ingot` → `Copper Plate`

## Custom Enchantments

### Excavation
`Excavation` is a Hammer-only custom enchantment.

- It can appear in an enchanting table when enchanting a `Hammer` or a normal `Book`.
- The resulting enchanted books are Hammer-only and cannot be applied to other tools.
- Matching levels can be combined in an anvil just like normal enchantments.

#### Excavation I
- Enables mining with the Hammer.
- Intended as the first step toward turning the Hammer into a utility mining tool.

#### Excavation II
- Enables `3x3` mining.
- Mines the targeted block plus the surrounding plane based on the face you are mining.
- Shows a preview overlay of the `3x3` area while aiming at a valid block in range.

## Enchanting Notes
- The enchanting table is restricted to Hammer enchant options for the Hammer and Hammer enchant books.
- A Hammer with `Excavation II` shows its mining preview only when you are close enough to actually hit the block.
- The preview copies the shape/data of the overlaid blocks so stairs, doors, slabs, and other shaped blocks are easier to read.

## Notes
The Hammer is the entry point for the whole tech tree.
Even after unlocking mining enchantments, it remains the main primitive processing tool for ores and plates.
