# Crushed Copper

## Summary
- **Tier:** Primitive Tech
- **Base item:** Copper Nugget
- **Purpose:** Intermediate copper material

## How to Obtain
### Anvil Processing
- `Hammer + Raw Copper` → `2x Crushed Copper`

## Smelting
- `Crushed Copper` → `Copper Ingot`

## Uses
- Early ore doubling path.
- Can also be made in larger amounts with the [Press](../machines/press.md).

## Press Processing
- `Raw Copper` in front of a powered Press → `4x Crushed Copper`
