# Crushed Iron

## Summary
- **Tier:** Primitive Tech
- **Base item:** Iron Nugget
- **Purpose:** Intermediate iron material

## How to Obtain
### Anvil Processing
- `Hammer + Raw Iron` → `2x Crushed Iron`

## Smelting
- `Crushed Iron` → `Iron Ingot`

## Uses
- Early ore doubling path.
- Can also be made in larger amounts with the [Press](../machines/press.md).

## Press Processing
- `Raw Iron` in front of a powered Press → `4x Crushed Iron`
