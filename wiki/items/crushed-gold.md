# Crushed Gold

## Summary
- **Tier:** Primitive Tech
- **Base item:** Gold Nugget
- **Purpose:** Intermediate gold material

## How to Obtain
### Anvil Processing
- `Hammer + Raw Gold` → `2x Crushed Gold`

## Smelting
- `Crushed Gold` → `Gold Ingot`

## Uses
- Early ore doubling path.
- Can also be made in larger amounts with the [Press](../machines/press.md).

## Press Processing
- `Raw Gold` in front of a powered Press → `4x Crushed Gold`
