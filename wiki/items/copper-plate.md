# Copper Plate

## Summary
- **Tier:** Primitive Tech
- **Base item:** Acacia Pressure Plate
- **Purpose:** Flattened copper component

## How to Obtain
### Anvil Processing
- `Hammer + Copper Ingot` → `Copper Plate`

### Press Processing
- `Copper Ingot` in front of a powered [Press](../machines/press.md) → `2x Copper Plate`

## Uses
- Copper Shaft recipe
- Future conductive machine parts
