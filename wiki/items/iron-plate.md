# Iron Plate

## Summary
- **Tier:** Primitive Tech
- **Base item:** Heavy Weighted Pressure Plate
- **Purpose:** Iron component for crafting and pressing

## How to Obtain
### Anvil Processing
- `Hammer + Iron Ingot` → `Iron Plate`

### Press Processing
- `Iron Ingot` in front of a powered [Press](../machines/press.md) → `2x Iron Plate`

## Uses
- Hand Crank recipe
- Iron Shaft recipe
- Press recipe
